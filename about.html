Our Journey Through Time

From a spark of an idea to a constellation of innovations, follow the path of Node Orbis.

2021 - Genesis

The Spark of Orbis

Abhinit Barua conceptualizes Node Orbis, laying the theoretical groundwork for fifth-dimensional processing and the initial suite of reality-bending products.

2022 - Formation

Core Nexus Assembled

Nihal Jyoti Das and Barnil Thakuria join the venture, bringing critical expertise in asset management and market strategy, solidifying the foundational team.

2024 - First Light

Project "Laughing Beasts" & "Kliff" Prototypes

Initial prototypes of The Laughing Beasts email protocol and Kliff multi-dimensional search engine demonstrate breakthrough capabilities, attracting early interest.

Present - Expansion

NeuroStem, TRATA, Bleenk OS Launch

Node Orbis expands its portfolio with NeuroStem, TRATA, and Bleenk OS, marking its official entry into diverse high-impact sectors and continuing its mission to engineer the future.
